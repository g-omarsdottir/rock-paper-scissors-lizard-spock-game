Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock - Game

Welcome to a game of Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock!

About this Game

The game Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock, often referred to as the RPSLS-Game for short, is based on the old classic "Rock, Paper, Scissors" game , but has the twist of two added game variables, a Lizard and Spock.

The RPSLS-Game gained popularity after being played on the TV sitcom The Big Bang Theory.

You can play this game with friends using hand gestures, or dive right in and play it here against the computer.

How to Play this Game

Choose one of the five options, Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, or Spock by clicking on the appropriate button.

Simultaniously, the computer's choice will be randomly generated.

Each option can beat or be beaten by two other options based on the game rules.

If you win, 1 point will be added to your score.

If both players, you and the computer, choose the same variable, it's a tie. Even though everybody wins, you will not gain a point.

If you lose, don't panic, you will not lose a point.

When either player reaches 10 points on the scoreboard, the ultimate game champion is declared.

Game Rules

  • Scissors cut Paper
  • Paper covers Rock
  • Rock crushes Lizard
  • Lizard poisons Spock
  • Spock smashes Scissors
  • Scissors decapitate Lizard
  • Lizard eats Paper
  • Paper disproves Spock
  • Spock vaporizes Rock
  • Rock crushes Scissors
