About this Game

The game Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock, often referred to as the RPSLS-Game for short, is just like the old classic "Rock, Paper, Scissors" game, but with a twist: a Lizard and Spock!

You may know this game from the TV sitcom The Big Bang Theory. If you don't, no worries, just keep reading, we've got you covered.

How to Play

Choose one of the five options, Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, or Spock by clicking on the appropriate button.

Simultaniously, the computer's choice will be randomly generated.

Each option can beat or be beaten by two other options based on the game rules.

If you win, 1 point will be added to your score.

If both players, you and the computer, choose the same variable, it's a tie. Even though everybody wins, you will not gain a point.

If you lose, don't panic, you will not lose a point.

When either player reaches 10 points on the scoreboard, the ultimate game champion is declared.

Forget coin flipping, this is how real decisions are made!

Game Rules

Game rules: Scissors cut Paper, Paper covers Rock, Rock crushes Lizard, Lizard poisons Spock, Spock smashes Scissors, Scissors decapitate Lizard, Lizard eats Paper, Paper disproves Spock, Spock vaporizes Rock, Rock crushes Scissors
